# Business Background

Supplier onboarding is a common procurement control point. Teams need to answer whether a supplier is real, compliant, operationally stable and suitable for the purchase exposure.

Common pain points:

- Evidence is scattered across registry pages, media, contracts and internal notes.
- Risk rules are written in policy documents but applied inconsistently.
- Procurement wants speed, while compliance needs explainability and audit trails.
- Interview demos often show generic agents, but not a realistic business workflow.

SupplyGuard Agent addresses this by turning onboarding into a repeatable workflow: collect evidence, retrieve policy, score risks, generate a report and capture human review.

The project intentionally uses mock data. That makes the demo reproducible, avoids sensitive company information and lets the candidate explain architecture instead of relying on live third-party APIs.
